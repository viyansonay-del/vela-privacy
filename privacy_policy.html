Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Vela ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application Vela ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

Vela is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Decision data: The decisions, descriptions, and notes you enter are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data is never sent to our servers or shared with third parties.
  • AI analysis: When you request an AI analysis, your decision text is sent encrypted to our AI backend (powered by Supabase Edge Functions) solely for the purpose of generating an analysis. This data is not stored on our servers after processing.
  • Purchase information: If you subscribe to Vela Premium, purchase transactions are handled by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat may collect anonymized purchase data as described in their privacy policy.
  • Language preference: Your selected language is stored locally on your device.
  • Name: If you choose to set a display name, it is stored locally on your device only.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not serve advertisements.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not store your decision data on any server.

3. Data Storage

All your decision data, notes, and settings are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted. We have no ability to recover this data.

4. AI Analysis

When you submit a decision for AI analysis, the text is transmitted securely (HTTPS) to our backend service. The data is used only to generate your analysis response and is not stored, logged, or used for training purposes.

5. In-App Purchases

Vela offers an optional Premium subscription. All payment processing is handled by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. Subscription management is handled via RevenueCat. Please review RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

6. Children's Privacy

Vela is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information. All data transmitted to our backend is encrypted using HTTPS. Your local data is protected by your device's built-in security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no data to access, correct, or delete from our side. You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app.
