Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Vela is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Decision data: Your decisions, descriptions, and notes are stored locally on your device by default. Only the specific text you choose to analyze is securely transmitted to our AI backend for processing.
  • AI analysis: When you request an AI analysis, the relevant text is sent via HTTPS to our backend (Supabase Edge Functions) solely to generate your analysis. We do not intentionally store your content after processing and do not use it for advertising or profiling.
  • Purchase information: Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple or Google. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status only.
  • Language & display name: Stored locally on your device only.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • No email address or persistent personal identifiers
  • No advertising or behavioral tracking SDKs
  • No advertisements are served
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for profiling or automated decision-making

3. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all data is permanently deleted. We have no ability to recover it.

4. AI Analysis

Decision text is transmitted securely (HTTPS) to our backend for analysis only. We do not intentionally store your content after processing and do not use it for advertising or profiling purposes. Standard infrastructure error logging may apply as part of normal backend operations.

5. In-App Purchases

All payments are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not collect payment information. Subscription management is via RevenueCat — see their policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

6. Children's Privacy

Vela is not intended for children under the age required by applicable local laws for consent to digital services (typically 13 in the US, 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Security

All data transmitted to our backend is encrypted via HTTPS. Your local data is protected by your device's built-in security.

8. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — AI backend infrastructure. supabase.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management. revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple / Google — Payment processing and app distribution.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be reflected by updating the date above. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance.

10. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is nothing to access or delete from our side. You can delete all local data by uninstalling the app.
